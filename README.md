# Global Markets Trade Idea & Portfolio Review Casebook

A no-code markets portfolio project focused on **FX, gold, and Brent-linked oil proxy analysis** through macro event case studies, annotated chart review, trade idea journaling, portfolio review, and disciplined risk management.

## What this project shows
This casebook was created to demonstrate a structured approach to:
- monitoring global financial markets
- interpreting macro and geopolitical events
- developing trade ideas
- reviewing portfolio exposure
- applying disciplined risk management
- evaluating outcomes through post-trade review

## Markets covered
- **FX:** EUR/USD
- **Precious Metals:** Gold (XAU/USD)
- **Energy Proxy:** BNO, used as a Brent-linked proxy due to platform instrument availability

## Case studies
- **Federal Reserve and Gold Reaction** — 28 January 2026
- **ECB Decision and EUR/USD Reaction** — 19 March 2026
- **OPEC+ Statement and Brent-Linked Proxy Reaction** — 1 March 2026
- **Geopolitical Conflict, Energy Shock, and Cross-Asset Market Reaction** — 1–20 March 2026

## Framework
The project is built around a repeatable process:
1. Market monitoring  
2. Trade idea generation  
3. Risk management  
4. Portfolio review  
5. Post-trade evaluation  

## Risk and review framework
- **0.5% risk per trade**
- **1:2 target risk-reward**
- scenario-based planning
- exposure and correlation review
- trade journal and weekly portfolio review summaries

## Project metrics
- Clients supported: **8**
- Average trades reviewed per week: **20**
- Portfolio range: **$2,500–$4,000**
- Asset classes covered: **FX and Commodities**
- Profit factor: **1.0**
- Sharpe ratio range: **0.5–1.0**
